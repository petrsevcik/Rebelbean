What is Test Roast?

In order for our coffees to be at the top of the production range at the moment of inclusion, we first need to test how they behave in the roaster and taste everything thoroughly. Or maybe we just occasionally want to try a different approach in order to make it even better. But sometimes it doesn't quite work out the way we want it too... Don't worry, it's not a burnt coal or orange under-roasted grain, we just haven't reached its full potential. Most of the time, you'll find these coffees in our team's homes, but our own consumption is not keeping up. So you can play this game with us too! Don't ask what kind of coffee it is, how or when it was roasted. It's probably a coffee we're still working on, maybe even a limited edition, who knows! Check it out!
